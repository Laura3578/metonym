Legal · Privacy

Privacy Policy.

Plain-language summary of what Metonym LLC collects, why, how long we keep it, and how you can ask us to change or delete it.

Effective date: 2026-05-16 · Last updated: 2026-05-16

1. Who we are

Metonym LLC ("Metonym," "we," "us," "our") is a Colorado limited liability company providing independent clinical evaluation of AI systems. Our principal place of business is Trinidad, Colorado, USA. This policy applies to metonym.health and any subdomain we operate.

Dr. Laura L. Walsh, PsyD operates a separate clinical psychotherapy and forensic consulting practice under Walsh Psychology. That practice has its own privacy practices and is not covered by this policy.

2. Information we collect

Information you give us

When you fill out the contact form on this site, we receive:

  • Your name
  • Your organization (optional)
  • Your role or title (optional)
  • Your email address
  • The type of engagement you're considering
  • The message you write describing what you want evaluated

If you email us directly at laura@metonym.health, we receive whatever you include in that message and your email metadata (sender address, subject, timestamps).

Information collected automatically

When you visit the site, our hosting provider receives standard server-log information: your IP address, browser type and version, operating system, the page you visited, the referring URL, and the date and time. We do not currently set our own cookies, do not run analytics, and do not embed tracking pixels.

What we don't collect

  • We do not collect health information about you personally.
  • We do not collect financial account information.
  • We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.
  • We do not sell your information to anyone, ever.

3. Service providers we use

We rely on a small set of third-party services to operate the site. These providers are "data processors" — they handle data on our behalf according to their own privacy practices:

  • Formspree — processes contact form submissions and forwards them to our inbox. Their privacy policy: formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy
  • GitHub Pages — hosts the site files. Their privacy policy: docs.github.com (GitHub privacy statement)
  • Google Fonts — serves the Manrope and Inter fonts. Their privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Porkbun — registrar and DNS for the domain. Their privacy policy: porkbun.com/privacy

We do not sell or rent your information to advertisers, marketers, or data brokers.

4. How we use your information

We use the information you give us to:

  • Respond to your inquiry and schedule any scoping call you request
  • Prepare a scoping memo or engagement letter if relevant
  • Maintain a record of business communications in the ordinary course of running a consulting practice
  • Comply with our legal obligations (including litigation hold, where applicable)

5. How long we keep it

We retain contact-form submissions and related email correspondence for as long as is reasonably necessary to evaluate, scope, and (where applicable) document an engagement. As a general matter, contact records that do not lead to an engagement are deleted or anonymized within 24 months of the last meaningful interaction. Engagement-related records are retained for the duration of the engagement plus any applicable professional, regulatory, or contractual retention period.

6. Your rights

Regardless of where you live, you can ask us to:

  • Access — tell you what information we have about you
  • Correct — fix anything that's wrong
  • Delete — remove information we no longer need (subject to any active legal or contractual retention)
  • Export — send you a copy in a portable format

To make a request, email laura@metonym.health with "Privacy Rights Request" in the subject line. We'll respond within 30 days.

If you're in the European Union or United Kingdom

Our legal basis for processing your contact-form information is our legitimate interest in responding to your inquiry and conducting our business (GDPR Article 6(1)(f)). You have the right to object to that processing at any time. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your national supervisory authority. We do not currently rely on cross-border data transfer mechanisms because our processing occurs in the United States; if you do not want your information processed in the US, please do not submit the contact form and instead contact us via other means.

If you're in California

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act / California Privacy Rights Act, you have rights to access, delete, correct, and limit use of your personal information, and the right to opt out of "sale" or "sharing" of personal information. We do not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising purposes. To exercise any right, contact us at the email above.

7. Security

We take commercially reasonable measures to protect your information, including limiting internal access to those who need it and relying on reputable third-party processors for transmission and storage. No method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security. If you have reason to believe your interaction with us is no longer secure, please contact us immediately.

8. Children

This site is intended for AI safety professionals, counsel, regulators, and others responsible for AI systems used in mental-health-adjacent contexts. It is not directed to children under 18, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Material changes will be disclosed by a prominent notice on this page for at least 30 days.

10. Contact

Privacy questions, rights requests, or complaints: laura@metonym.health with "Privacy" in the subject line.

Postal: Metonym LLC, Trinidad, Colorado, USA. Full street address available on request.
